Retail Return & Exchange Policy (Rev. 5 — applies differently across departments)

1. Standard Return Window  
   - Items may be returned within 30 days if unused.  
   - Electronics must be returned within 14 days unless marked “Extended Return Eligible.”  
   - “Extended Return Eligible” items have a return window of 21–45 days depending on SKU series.

2. Final Sale & Clearance  
   - Items marked FINAL SALE cannot be returned or refunded.  
   - Exception: defective items MAY be accepted depending on manager approval.  
   - Exception to exception: certain “as-is” items are excluded even when defective.

3. Defective or Damaged Items  
   - Defective items can be returned anytime within the manufacturer warranty period.*
   *Note: warranty durations vary and some are not tracked in our system.

   - If the defect is determined to be “minor cosmetic,” refunds may not apply.  
   - If defect is on arrival, returns “should be accepted” even outside the window unless overloaded.

4. Documentation & Conditions  
   - Receipt is usually required, but may be waived if paid with store account.  
   - Photo evidence may substitute for physical inspection “when workload is high.”

5. Regional Adjustments  
   - Some regions require refunds for defective items beyond 60 days.  
   - The EU division recommends accepting defective-item returns up to 90 days.  
   - These practices are not consistently implemented across stores.
